How to Use Adinkra Alive

1
Allow camera access when prompted.
2
Point your camera at any Adinkra symbol - on fabric, in books, or printed images.
3
Hold steady for best detection results. The app will identify the symbol and display its meaning.
4
Tap "Capture Frame" if automatic detection doesn't work right away.

How to Use ARIA Ghana

Follow these simple steps to immerse yourself in our augmented reality experience

1

Grant Camera Access

When prompted, allow the application to access your device's camera. This is essential for the AR experience to work properly.

2

Wait for Scanning Animation

After granting camera access, wait for the scanning animation to appear. This may take a moment depending on your internet connection speed.

3

Scan the Artwork

Point your camera at the artwork to scan it. Keep your device steady and ensure good lighting for the best experience.

4

Enjoy the Experience

Once the artwork is recognized, the AR experience will begin automatically. Move around to explore different perspectives.
